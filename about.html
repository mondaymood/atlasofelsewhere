Hey, my name
is Tara.

I was born in beautiful Canada, but grew up in Germany. After graduating from design school, I spent the next six years making my way from intern to Senior Art Director at an award winning design agency in Düsseldorf. Sounds like a pretty straightforward resume. That is until my husband and I decided to pack up our life and move across the globe. Which is when I started this location independent design studio and travel blog from our current base in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Xin chào!

Even though I am currently based in Vietnam, I am open to travel within Asia for your project. Send me a message so we can discuss details.
