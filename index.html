Hey, my name is Tara.
Nice to meet you!

Branding is my favorite part of design. A thoughtful and consistent brand elevates the customer's experience, connects you with your target audience and helps you reach your business goals. After 6 years working in design agencies I realized, that you don’t necessarily need an office to construct unique and strategic brands. Luckily I can take my creativity and experience wherever I go – which leads us to Atlas of Elsewhere, a space where I combine my two passions: Design & Travel.

Travel is my source of inspiration.

Discovering new cities, exploring museums, trying new food and learning about different cultures – this is where my inspiration comes from. Design plays an important part – while deciding on hotels, restaurant or shops I always try to spot the most aesthetic place (while keeping it budget-friendly). If you love travelling as much as me I hope I can give you some valuable insight for your next trip.
